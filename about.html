Together we can reverse climate change

More and more organizations are aiming for net-zero emissions. Land and forest restoration are proven practices to remove carbon from the atmosphere. Oasis connects carbon sequestration solutions to organizations, who are ready for investing in CO2-removal.


Why should we care?

The news on climate change keep pouring in, it doesn't look too good.

  • Climate change threatens human life on earth
  • The climate crisis needs to be addressed urgently

Greenhouse gas emissions reductions are not enough to reach the Paris Agreement, the efforts need to be complemented by solutions for removing already emitted CO2 from the atmosphere. Soils and biomass hold huge potential to sink CO2.

What can we do about it?

Land sinks hold a significant carbon sequestration potential. The world is ready for forest and landscape restoration. Research shows a trillion trees could be planted. According to Project Drawdown the land sink sector can reduce and sequester 243.1-387.8 Gigatons of CO2e between 2020–2050. Oasis helps organization turn climate action into a competive advantage.
